Example teaching approach

# Teaching approach

## What to teach
Orwell’s argument about clear writing and political language.

## Aims of teaching
Learners should be able to explain Orwell’s main criticism and revise unclear sentences.

## Who is being taught
A-level English students with some experience of argumentative essays.

## How to teach
Use close reading, short examples, guided discussion, and sentence-level rewriting practice.
