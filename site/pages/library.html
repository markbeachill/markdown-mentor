Pedagogy specifications

A Pedagogy Specification sets how the material is taught: tone, level, support, practice, and feedback.

School level

Clear explanations, guided practice, frequent short tasks, quick specific feedback.

ESL / EFL

Graded language, pre-taught vocabulary, controlled-to-free practice, gentle correction.

Undergraduate

Discussion, evidence evaluation, argument, and independent thinking.

Primary

Very warm and simple, high support, short playful hands-on tasks.

Vocational

Practical and direct, demonstrate then practise, check competence to standard.

Style profiles

A style profile controls how Markdown parts become document styles, such as heading sizes and colours. They are plain JSON files. You can build your own on the Style page without editing JSON by hand.

Larger body text and a blue heading colour, suited to handouts students read.

Teacher notes

A serif body font and a purple heading colour, to tell teacher notes apart from student materials.

Build your own

Use the Style builder to choose fonts and colours and download a ready profile.
