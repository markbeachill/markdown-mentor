Workflow / Step 4

Step 4

Decide what to teach

Who leads this step: You + AI

Teaching and learning view

This step protects learners from vague or overloaded materials. A good brief says what matters now, who the learners are, and what should be left out.

WhatWrite a Teaching Brief yourself, or ask the AI to suggest possible briefs from the Markdown library/content pack. Choose one and edit it.
WhyThe same source pack can support many lessons. Without a brief, the AI may try to cover everything at once.
NextSave the chosen brief, then choose the pedagogy that matches the learners.

What to do

  1. State the audience and level.
  2. Name the learning purpose in plain English.
  3. List what must be included and what should be excluded.
  4. Keep the brief small enough for the materials you actually want to make.

Check before moving on

Ask: would a teacher know exactly what to teach after reading this brief?
