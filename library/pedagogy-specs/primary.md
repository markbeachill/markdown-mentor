# Pedagogy Specification: Primary

A Pedagogy Specification explains *how* the material should be taught. This one
is for primary-age children. Edit it to match your class.

## Learner type

Young children, usually with an adult guiding the lesson.

## Level

Beginner. Assume little or no prior knowledge and short attention spans.

## Tone

Very warm and simple. Very short sentences. Familiar words and friendly
examples.

## Amount of scaffolding

Very high. Model every step. Use pictures, repetition, and clear routines.

## Practice style

Short, playful, hands-on tasks. Lots of doing, not much reading. Frequent
changes of activity.

## Feedback style

Immediate and encouraging. Praise effort. Keep corrections gentle and specific.

## Assessment style

Quick games and simple checks. Avoid long written questions.

## Broad teaching approach

Show, do together, do alone, celebrate. Keep every task short and clear.
