# Pedagogy Specification: Vocational and workplace

A Pedagogy Specification explains *how* the material should be taught. This one
is for vocational training and workplace learning. Edit it to match your trade
or setting.

## Learner type

Learners training for a job or skill, often adults who want practical results.

## Level

Mixed. Some are complete beginners to the skill; some have experience.

## Tone

Practical and direct. Connect every idea to the job it is for.

## Amount of scaffolding

Moderate to high, reducing as the learner gains skill. Demonstrate first, then
let the learner do it.

## Practice style

Realistic tasks that match the job. Step-by-step procedures, checklists, and
safety points where relevant.

## Feedback style

Clear and immediate, focused on doing the task correctly and safely.

## Assessment style

Can the learner do the task to standard? Use practical checks and competence
checklists, not just written tests.

## Broad teaching approach

Explain why it matters, demonstrate, supervise practice, then check competence.
