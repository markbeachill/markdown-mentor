# Teaching Materials List: Revision Pack

A Teaching Materials List is a chosen set of material types for one purpose.
This "Revision Pack" list is for learners going back over a topic they have
already met.

Materials to create:

- A summary (the key points on one page).
- A glossary (key terms to recall).
- A revision quiz (ten or fewer questions).
- An answer key (kept separate from the quiz).

Why these four: revision works best with a quick reminder, then active recall,
then a way to check answers honestly.
