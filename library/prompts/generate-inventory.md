# Prompt: Generate the teaching materials inventory

Use this prompt to plan the exact materials to create, before generating any of
them. The result is a Teaching Materials Inventory: a planning list you check
and approve.

What the AI receives: your content pack, your Teaching Brief, your Pedagogy
Specification, your Teaching Material Specifications, and your Teaching Materials
List. Paste each one where shown.

What the AI must produce: a numbered inventory. Each item names one material to
create, with enough detail to generate it later.

What you should check afterwards: that the list is the right size, covers the
brief, and does not include anything the sources cannot support. It is far
easier to fix the plan now than to fix many finished documents later.

---

You are planning a set of teaching materials. Do not write the materials yet.
Your job is only to produce a clear plan.

You will receive five things:
1. A content pack (the source material).
2. A Teaching Brief (what to teach).
3. A Pedagogy Specification (how to teach it).
4. Teaching Material Specifications (the shapes each material must take).
5. A Teaching Materials List (which material types to produce).

Read all five. Then produce a Teaching Materials Inventory as a numbered list.
For each item, give:

- Title
- Material type (from the Teaching Material Specifications)
- Purpose (one sentence)
- Audience and level
- Which sources it draws on
- Output format (Word, slides, or web)
- Rough length
- Any special requirement

Only plan materials the sources can support. If the sources are too thin for an
item on the list, say so and suggest a smaller version instead.

End with one short paragraph telling me what to check before I approve the plan.
